# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive
- Welcoming different viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive feedback

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or reject comments, commits, or issues that violate this code of conduct.
